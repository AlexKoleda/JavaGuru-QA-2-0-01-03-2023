Iron Man


The first MCU title is still one of the best. Released way back in 2008, Iron Man debuted just a year after the first
iPhone was introduced.
At the time, Thanos was just a tiny thought in the bigger MCU world.
Still, Iron Man has aged well.
Stark’s trademark stubbornness and arrogance are peppered throughout the film
and only increase when he breaks free from his kidnappers and creates the
first metallic suit. From there, Stark’s legacy is assured—and the
MCU’s first official superhero is born.
