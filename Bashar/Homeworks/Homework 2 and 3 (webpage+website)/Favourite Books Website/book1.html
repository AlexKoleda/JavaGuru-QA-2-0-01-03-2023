The Three Body      Problem
by Liu Cixin


Liu Cixin is a Chinese computer engineer and science fiction writer. He is a nine-time winner of China's Galaxy Award and has also received the 2015 Hugo Award for his novel The Three-Body Problem as well as the 2017 Locus Award for Death's End. He is also a winner of the Chinese Nebula Award.


Originally published: 2008
The Three-Body Problem is a science fiction novel.
Hugo Award for Best Novel
Nebula Award Nomination for Best Novel


The title refers to the three-body problem in orbital mechanics.
It is the first novel of the Remembrance of Earth's Past trilogy.
