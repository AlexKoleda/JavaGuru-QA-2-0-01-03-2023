Contact
by Carl Sagan


Carl Sagan was an astronomer, planetary scientist, cosmologist, astrophysicist, astrobiologist, author, and science communicator. He assembled the first physical message sent into space, universal messages that could be understood by any extraterrestrial intelligence.


Contact is a 1985 hard science fiction novel. The novel originated as a screenplay; when development of the film stalled, Sagan decided to convert the stalled film into a novel. The film concept was subsequently revived and eventually released in 1997 as the film Contact starring Jodie Foster.
